Gao   Yang

Gao Yang，male，a member of the Communist Party of China and an associate professor at Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts


Specializing in Chinese flower and bird painting theory and creation research. He is also a member of the Shaanxi Provincial Artists Association and a young artist. Gao graduated from the Chinese Painting

Program at Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts in July 2014, after which he remained at the institution. In 2018, he was admitted to the doctoral program in Chinese Painting at Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts, studied under Professor Jiang Yixiang.
高   杨

男，中国共产党党员
西安美术学院教师，副教授


陕西省美术家协会会员
专业方向：中国花鸟画创作与理论研究


2014年7月毕业于西安美术学院中国画专业同年7月留校，2018年考入西安美术学院中国画专业博士研究生，师从姜怡翔教授。



In 2011, his work "Men at Shehuo①" won the Excellent Word Award at the First Shaanxi Province University Student Exhibition organized by the Shaanxi Provincial Artists Association;

In 2013, Gao was awarded a scholarship by the "Deutsch——Chinesischer Kulturaustausch für Kunst und Design e.V."(DCKD) to pursue studies and cultural exchange in Germany. During this period, his work "Window" series was featured in the German-Chinese Art Exchange Exhibition.

In 2014 graduation piece "Call from After" was awarded the Third Prize for Master's Graduation Works, as well as the Liu Wenxi Award and the Red Star Award at Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts.

In 2014, the artwork "Call from After" was chosen to be exhibited at the "Art Exhibition Commemorating the 65th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China in Shaanxi Province", which was organized by the Shaanxi Provincial Department of Culture, Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Shaanxi Provincial Artists Association. It was also selected to participate in the "12th National Art Exhibition".

In 2014, the artwork "Valentine's Day" was chosen to be exhibited at the "Art Exhibition Commemorating the 65th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China in Shaanxi Province", which was organized by the Shaanxi Provincial Department of Culture, Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Shaanxi Provincial Artists Association.



In October 2019, the artwork "Silk Road Impressions - Rainforest" was chosen to be part of the National Art Fund's sponsored project for the "Belt-and-Road" Chinese Painting Talent Development Program exhibition;

In 2020, the artwork "Unity is Strength" was selected to be part of the Descendants of Dragon - Gengzi Year Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan and Overseas Artists Memorial Ceremony for Xuanyuan Huangdi Online Calligraphy and Painting Art Exhibition;

2021, the artwork "Impressions of Saihanba" was selected for the art exhibition celebrating the 100th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China in Shaanxi Province;

2022, the work "Happy Time" was selected into the Youth · Footprints - National Art College students theme creation exhibition;

Gao's work "Shattered Reflection in the Water - Forward" was published in "Beauty and Times";2022 Works "Qinling Mountains - The Eye of the Secrets" and "Saihanba Impression" published in China Publishing Journal (CSSCI);

In 2022, Gao participated in the creation of the book world classic pop-up book rare edition "Datang Chang 'an" in 2021 Xi'an Excellent cultural and creative products, and rated as "Gold Medal".


① "shehuo" literally translates as "Earth and Fire" and is a festival mostly celebrated in the countryside.
2011年作品《社火人》获得陕西省美协组织的陕西省第一届大学生展优秀作品奖；

2013年获得“中德艺术设计交流中心”（DCKD）的奖学金，前往德国交流学习。此期间作品《窗》系列参加德中艺术交流展；

2014年毕业作品《远方的呼唤》获得西安美术学院硕士毕业作品三等奖、刘文西奖、红星奖等；

2014年作品《远方的呼唤》入选由陕西省文化厅，陕西省文学艺术界联合会，陕西省美术家协会主办的《陕西省庆祝中华人民共和国成立65周年美术作品展览》，并被推选参加《第十二届全国美术作品展览》；

2014年作品《情人节》入选由陕西省文化厅，陕西省文学艺术界联合会，陕西省美术家协会主办的《陕西省庆祝中华人民共和国成立65周年美术作品展览》；







2019年10月《丝路印象——雨林》入选国家艺术基金，2019年度资助项目“‘一带一路’中国画创作人才培养项目”作品展；

2020年作品《众志成城》入选龙的传人——庚子年港澳台海外艺术家公祭轩辕黄帝网络书画艺术展；

2021年作品《塞罕坝印象》入选陕西省庆祝中国共产党成立100周年美术作品展；

2022年作品《欢乐时光》入选青春·足迹——全国艺术院校学生主题创作展；作品《水中划影——前进》刊登于《美与时代》；

2022年作品《秦岭——秘境之眼》和作品《塞罕坝印象》刊登于《中国出版》（CSSCI）;

2022年参与创作书籍世界经典立体书珍藏版《大唐长安》，在2021年西安市优秀文创产品评为“金奖”。
